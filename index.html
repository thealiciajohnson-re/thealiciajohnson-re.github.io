Your Roadmap

The Steps, and How I Help

Buying and selling can feel overwhelming. Here's exactly what to expect—and how I support you through each step.

1

Get Pre-Approved

Before house hunting, you'll connect with a lender to understand your budget and strengthen your offers.

How I help: I connect you with trusted local lenders and explain exactly what your pre-approval means for your search.
2

Define Your Wish List

We clarify your must-haves, nice-to-haves, preferred neighborhoods, and timeline.

How I help: I set you up with a personalized home search so new listings reach you the moment they hit the market.
3

Tour Homes

We visit properties together (in person or virtually) and evaluate each one against your goals.

How I help: I schedule showings, point out value and red flags, and give you honest, expert perspective on every home.
4

Make an Offer

When you find the one, we craft a competitive offer backed by current market data.

How I help: I run a comparative market analysis and negotiate hard on your behalf to win the home at the right price and terms.
5

Inspections & Due Diligence

Once your offer is accepted, we inspect the property and review all disclosures.

How I help: I coordinate inspections, interpret the reports, and negotiate repairs or credits when needed.
6

Close & Get Your Keys

We finalize your loan, complete the paperwork, and you officially become a homeowner.

How I help: I manage the timeline with escrow and your lender, keep everything on track, and hand you the keys with a smile.
1

Free Home Valuation

We start by determining what your home is truly worth in today's market.

How I help: I prepare a detailed comparative market analysis so you know exactly what to expect—no guesswork.
2

Prep & Stage

We make your home shine with repairs, decluttering, and staging that attracts buyers.

How I help: I create a custom prep plan and connect you with stagers, cleaners, and contractors from my trusted network.
3

Professional Marketing

Your home is photographed, listed, and promoted to reach the right buyers.

How I help: I invest in professional photography, online syndication, and targeted social media to maximize your home's exposure.
4

Showings & Open Houses

Qualified buyers tour your home and we gather feedback in real time.

How I help: I host open houses, manage all showings, and keep you updated on interest and offers as they come in.
5

Review & Negotiate Offers

We evaluate every offer—not just price, but terms, timing, and buyer strength.

How I help: I walk you through each offer's pros and cons and negotiate to protect your bottom line and your timeline.
6

Escrow & Closing

We navigate inspections, appraisal, and paperwork through to a successful close.

How I help: I manage every deadline, troubleshoot issues before they become problems, and get you to the closing table with confidence.
